About

Symbiosis Institute of Technology, Pune proudly welcomes you to SYMBITECH 2022, the annual technical festival, organized on the 28th and 29th of September, 2022.

SYMBITECH will help young minds, broaden their vision, and surrender to their instincts for knowledge.

The theme of this technical fest would revolve around
COSMOS!

SYMBITECH is all set to blow up everyone's mind with lots off challenging events and knowledge hunts.
